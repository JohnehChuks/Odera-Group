About Us

About Us

Odera Training Academy offers a comprehensive range of consultancy services and training in inspection, Non-destructive testing (NDT), coatings, examinations, and Level 3 services to a global clientele. Through collaboration with the international organisation IMechE Argyll Ruane, we are dedicated to advancing and supporting quality inspection technologies, both in the field and through online platforms for schools and organisations.

By integrating the esteemed values of our partners, the Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE), with decades of industry expertise, we deliver professional, top-tier services. Our mission is to foster industry advancement by cultivating skilled and proficient professionals and providing ongoing support to both individuals and their employers throughout their careers. 

Join our Academy.
With a spirit of innovation, we navigate uncharted territories, exploring new ideas, technologies, and approaches.
Vision Statement

To be the leading Non-Destructive Testing [NDT] and software training academy in Africa 

Mission Statement

To graduate entry-level NDT engineers and technology professionals who excel in the fundamentals of their respective fields, and subsequently have successful careers in the oil and gas and technology industries. Their actions raise industry standards and surpass client expectations.

Why Odera Training Academy?

Level 3 Consultancy Services

Our Level 3 consultants, in collaboration with our partner IMechE Argyll Ruane, boast a combined work experience exceeding 100 years, encompassing various industries including:

- Nuclear
- Aerospace
- Petrochemical
- Oil & Gas

We extend our support to clients worldwide, aiding them in operating efficiently and in accordance with industry standards. 

Apprenticeship

Inspection and NDT Apprenticeships:
We support the delivery of inspection and NDT apprenticeships in partnership with local organisations and key government agencies in Nigeria. These include:

- API Inspectors
- Coating Inspectors
- Level 2 NDT Operators
- Level 3 Engineering Technicians
- Welding Inspectors 

Empowering Careers, Building Networks

At Odera Academy, we prioritise transparency. Our programs offer essential knowledge without hidden charges, designed to fast-track your entry into non-destructive testing and software fields. Duration varies by program.

Additionally, Odera Training Academy provides robust career support and networking opportunities. Engage with professionals from diverse sectors through our annual paid seminars and conferences. Stay informed and connected with the latest advancements in your field.
